Upon reflection, I would probably pursue art full-time if it payed the bills. I would probably continue to focus on immersive electronic installation art or film.
What I do artistically is inspired by at least one of two themes: comedy and science.
What excites me about art and science is the potential to embed scientifically derived knowledge in art pieces.
What excites me about comedy is making people laugh.
I've made short films, written and performed comedy, and had a couple of independent exhibits in the vein of new media / installation art, and I hope to again someday!

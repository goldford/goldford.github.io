Upon reflection, I would probably pursue art full-time if it payed the bills - at least until I missed science! I'm a flip-flopper. I would probably continue to focus on immersive electronic installation art or film. What I do artistically is inspired by at least one of two themes: comedy and science.
- What excites me about art and science: the potential to embed scientifically derived knowledge in art pieces.
- What excites me about comedy: making people laugh.
I've written, produced, directed, and acted in several short films that were screened at film festivals. My collaborators and I were super jazzed to once have our weird comedy shorts be featured by the JASH (Sarah Silverman, Michael Cera, Tim and Eric, and Reggie Watts) 'Buh' comedy channel.
I've also written and performed comedy, and had a couple of independent exhibits in the vein of new media / installation art - and I hope to again someday!
Some of what I've done has been a complete and utter failure. Some of it I'm very proud of!
Stay tuned for some Augmented Reality which I'm collaborating on with Sebastian Harder
